Privacy Policy for FuelRadar Asia

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

FuelRadar Asia (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the FuelRadar Asia mobile application (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Mobile wallet details (such as GCash) for rewards redemption

1.2 Location Information

FuelRadar Asia collects location data to:

  • Show nearby gas stations
  • Allow users to submit fuel prices based on location
  • Improve the accuracy of fuel price reports

Location data is only collected while the app is in use.

1.3 Fuel Price Submissions

  • Fuel price data
  • Gas station name and location
  • Photos of fuel price boards (if uploaded)
  • Date and time of submission

1.4 Device Information

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Anonymous usage data (analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the app
  • Display nearby fuel prices
  • Verify submitted fuel prices
  • Reward users for valid submissions
  • Improve app features and performance
  • Send notifications and important updates
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and abuse

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Sharing of Information

  • Cloud service providers (data storage)
  • Analytics providers (app improvement)
  • Payment providers (for rewards such as GCash)
  • Law enforcement if required by law

We do not share your personal data with advertisers.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Delete your account
