Account Deletion Request – FuelRadar Asia

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

If you would like to delete your FuelRadar Asia account and associated data, you may request account deletion through the following methods:

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email us at fuelsave.app@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your Name
    • Your Email Address used in FuelRadar Asia
    • Your Phone Number (if registered)

We will process your request within 7–14 business days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information
  • Your personal details (name, email, phone number)
  • Your reward points and rewards history
  • Your submitted fuel price history associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

We may retain certain data if required for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes. Submitted fuel price data may be kept for analytics but will no longer be associated with your personal account.
